StartMenu Catalog        

Note: This list is in perpetual continuous improvement and is therefore subject to frequent changes. It is provided here as reference only.   


This is the system that SetupS uses to determine or "map" a Catalog entry into into one's chosen advanced Startmenu Style (ssTek/Kazz/Custom). They are defined in a standard INI-type of database file called "Definitions.ini" which is located in the respective advanced menu sub-folder under the "Tools" folder.


Within a standard INI-file each property is defined by a "key" and a "value", delimited by an equals sign (=). The "key" appears to the left of the equals sign and the "value" to the right of it. Properties may be grouped into arbitrarily named "sections". The "section" name appears on a line by itself, in square brackets ([ and ]).


In the "Definitions.ini" file, each property is defined such that the "key" represents a particular Catalog entry and the "value" represents the actual location within the advanced Startmenu itself. The remainder of the file is devoted to detailing each advanced Startmenu location by way of the "sections"; but so far, only icons are defined at the moment.


Finally, an exclamation (!) is used at the beginning of a "key" to mean that that particular Catalog entry is being renamed or forwarded to another Catalog entry.
