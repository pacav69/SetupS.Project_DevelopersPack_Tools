ssCleaner Options        


Dud-shortcut removal:
(Global options for all SetupS tools.)

    • Disabled. Do not perform any ssCleaning functions.
    • Startmenu-only. Limits the ssCleaning location to only the Startmenu.
    • All Dud Shortcuts. All shortcut locations such as Desktop, Sendto, & QuickLaunch will be included in the ssCleaning.
    • Check for removed Startmenu items. ssCleaning is automatically performed whenever shortcuts are removed such as during Startmenu Style changes. (Recommended to leave this "on").
  • Remove Folders:
    (Global options for all SetupS tools.)
    • Dud Installed-to paths. Whether or not to remove the install-to paths referenced by dud shortcuts.
    • Uninstalled (deep scan). Do a deep-scan of uninstalled apps or games and remove their folders.
  • Instant ssCleaning: Perform ssCleaning right now.
