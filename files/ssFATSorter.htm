ssFATSorter. Alphanumerically sort files on drives that use a FAT file system. Especially useful to sort the files and folders on a USB flash drive or other portable media having a FAT12, FAT16, or FAT32 formatted disk. This works well on the devices that read them such as MP3 players, DivX/Xvid Video set-tops, Lynx, Muro, I-Bead, or your mobile phone flash card, etc.

Useful to sort these FAT drives before using ssWPI/ssGooey.
